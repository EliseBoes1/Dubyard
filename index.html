Welkom bij Dubyard!

Dubyard is a yearly free outdoor reggae music- and cultural event on the edge of the forest in Blanden (Oud-Heverlee) including workshops, sports and art. The mission of the event is to spread consciousness and positivity and to bring together a diverse group of people. Dubyard exhibits both soundsystem and live music, and guarantees good vibes at its beautiful natural location. Interactive educational workshops serve to promote a healthy, ecological, do-it-yourself lifestyle. However, Dubyard is also ideal for those simply looking to relax.
